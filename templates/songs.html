the tiny-mini music recommendation engine.

Lets say you want to dedicate a song with the words -I like blue eyes- but, you don't know any song that has those words in its lyrics. Muzix Dedicat.e can help you search for songs that has those words in its lyrics.
Just enter the words you want to be found in a song, hit the Submit button & we'll find the songs for you.
We'll even generate links you can use to go & listen to those songs we found.
